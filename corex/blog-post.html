Turning Properties Into Market Leaders

How we helped a luxury villa owner in Palm Jumeirah increase their RevPAR by 35% while maintaining 85%+ occupancy year-round.

A

Ahmed Al-Rashid

Property Owner

| January 10, 2026

The Challenge

When Ahmed Al-Rashid first approached CORE, he was managing a stunning 5-bedroom villa in Palm Jumeirah. Despite the property's prime location and luxurious amenities, he was struggling to achieve consistent occupancy and optimal revenue. His average occupancy rate hovered around 60%, and he was leaving significant money on the table due to static pricing that didn't account for market fluctuations.

"I knew my property had potential," Ahmed recalls, "but I didn't have the time or expertise to optimize pricing across multiple platforms while also managing guest communications and operations."

The Solution

CORE implemented a comprehensive revenue management strategy tailored specifically to Ahmed's property and the Dubai market. Our approach included:

1. Dynamic Pricing Implementation

We deployed our AI-powered pricing engine that analyzes over 50 data points in real-time, including competitor rates, local events, seasonal demand, and booking patterns. This allowed us to adjust prices multiple times per day to capture maximum revenue.

2. Multi-Channel Distribution

We optimized Ahmed's presence across Airbnb, Booking.com, and our direct booking platform, ensuring maximum visibility while preventing double bookings through unified calendar management.

3. Professional Listing Optimization

Our team enhanced the property's listings with professional photography, compelling descriptions, and strategic keyword placement to improve search rankings.

The Results

Within just six months of partnering with CORE, Ahmed saw remarkable improvements:

  • 35% increase in RevPAR — Revenue per available room grew significantly through optimized pricing
  • 85%+ occupancy rate — Up from 60% through strategic pricing and improved visibility
  • 4.9 average guest rating — Maintained through professional operations and guest communication
  • Zero operational headaches — Ahmed now focuses on growing his portfolio while we handle day-to-day operations

Key Takeaways

Ahmed's success story highlights several important lessons for holiday home operators in Dubai:

Dynamic pricing is essential. Static pricing simply cannot capture the full revenue potential of a property in a market as dynamic as Dubai. Our AI-powered system ensures prices are always optimized for current market conditions.

Professional management pays for itself. While some owners hesitate to partner with a management company, the revenue increase typically more than covers the management fees — often by a significant margin.

Guest experience drives revenue. By maintaining high service standards and responding quickly to guest needs, we were able to secure excellent reviews that further boosted the property's visibility and booking rate.
