FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

What's included in the management fee?

Our management fee covers everything from listing optimization and dynamic pricing to guest communication and performance reporting. Housekeeping and maintenance are available as add-ons or included in higher-tier plans.

Are there any setup fees?

No, there are no setup fees. We believe in earning your business through results, not upfront costs.

How do I get paid?

You receive your rental income directly from the booking platforms (Airbnb, Booking.com, etc.). Our management fee is invoiced monthly based on your booking revenue.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, our agreements are month-to-month with a 30-day notice period. We believe in earning your business every month through exceptional service and results.

What properties do you work with?

We work with holiday homes, vacation rentals, and short-term rental properties across Dubai. All properties must meet our quality standards and comply with local regulations.
